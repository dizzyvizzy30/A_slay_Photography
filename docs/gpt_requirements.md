# **AI Photography Coach – Mobile App Specification**

## **Overview**

AI Photography Coach is a mobile application designed to assist photographers end-to-end throughout their day-to-day workflow—from setting up an event space to receiving post-processing suggestions for event photos. The app operates as an **AI agent space**, allowing users to ask specific questions or upload images and receive tailored, actionable recommendations. The tool integrates with Instagram to automatically update the user’s photography page with curated posts, captions, and optimized hashtags.

The essential outputs the app provides include:

* **Suggested shots & poses**
* **Ideal camera settings tailored to the environment & event**
* **Mood-based post-processing recommendations**
* **Skill-level-specific guidance (beginner → pro)**

Target audience includes:

* Event photographers (weddings, corporate, concerts, astro)
* Students learning photography
* Hobbyist photographers seeking assistance
* Social media photographers needing professional-grade ideas and content
* Creators who want help generating articles, captions, or portfolio content

---

## **Key Features**

**Setup Analyzer** – Upload a photo of your event setup and the AI analyzes lighting, environment, and layout to suggest optimal shooting conditions and settings.

**Camera Settings Recommender** – Based on the setup and event type, the app recommends ISO, aperture, shutter speed, and lens choices.

**Shot & Pose Suggestions** – Generates ideas for shots, angles, and poses tailored to your venue, event type, and mood.

**Photography League Selector** – Adjusts advice for beginner, intermediate, or professional skill levels.

**Event-Based Shot Planner / Suggester** – Prebuilt templates for weddings, portraits, concerts, corporate events, etc., auto-customized to your setup.

**Mood-Based Post-Processing Guide** – Suggests editing styles, color grading, and Lightroom/Photoshop adjustments for a selected mood.

**Custom Shot Requests** – Users can prompt any type of shot (“romantic couple portraits,” “cinematic wide angles”) and get tailored recommendations.

**AI Portfolio Coach** – Analyzes past work and suggests which images should be included in the photographer’s professional portfolio.

**AI Caption Generator** – Creates captions in multiple styles (professional, luxury, emotional, minimalist, playful).

**Auto Hashtag Optimizer** – Suggests hashtags based on event type, mood, location, and niche for maximum reach.

**Standby Feature: Auto-Create Social Media Posts From Photos** – Takes user photo folders and generates ready-to-post social media content (layouts, captions, hashtags), with scheduling for Instagram updates.
